By public transport

Vienna Airport to Bratislava Airport

There are regular buses (Slovak Lines) from Vienna airport to Bratislava airport, tickets can be purchases on the bus.

Bratislava Airport to Bratislva train station

There is a bus (#61) from Bratislava airport to the train station, tickets can be purchased at the bus stop.

Bratislava train station to Ruzomberok

The nearest train station to the venue is Ruzomberok, there are trains (www.zsr.sk) to Ruzomberok from Bratislava approx. every two hours.

Ruzomberok to Donovaly

If you are travelling via public transport, let us know ahead of time and we'll send someone to pick you up at the train station and bring you to the venue.
