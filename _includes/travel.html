Airports

Bratislava, Slovakia

Bratislava is approx. 3.5 hours by car to the venue, and is served by Ryanair who offer daily direct flights from Dublin and other locations.

Vienna, Austria.

Vienna is approx. 4 hours by car to the venue and is served by Aer Lingus (daily direct flights from Dublin) and other international carriers.

By car

Care hire is available at both airports, and also in city centre locations. We recommend travelling on the motorway via Zilina, rather than the shorter non-motorway route usually provided by Satnavs. Directions via Zilina on Google Maps.
